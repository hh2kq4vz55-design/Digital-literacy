Part B – Prevention Checklist (Stay Safe Online)
Never click on suspicious email or SMS links
Check sender email IDs carefully
Do not share OTP, passwords, or PINs
Enable two-factor authentication
Use strong and unique passwords
Avoid public Wi-Fi for banking


UPI Safety Tips:


Always verify UPI requests before approving
Never accept “collect requests” from unknown users


Report Cybercrime:


National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
